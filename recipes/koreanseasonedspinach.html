Korean Seasoned Spinach

Korean seasoned spinach is a simple healthy sidedish.

Ingredients

  • Spinach
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Olive Oil

Recipe Instructions

  1. Trim the roots off the spinach and wash it thoroughly
  2. Blanch the spinach in salted boiling water for 20-30 seconds until wilted
  3. Remove the spinach from the pot and shock in icy cold water
  4. Drain and squeeze out excess water
  5. Cut into 8cm pieces
  6. Mix in olive oil, sesame seeds, and sesame oil and serve
