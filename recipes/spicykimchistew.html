Spicy Kimchi Stew

Quick, tasty, and healthy, spicy kimchi stew is a satisfying meal

Ingredients

  • Kimchi
  • Fatty Pork Ribs
  • Korean Red Chili Pepper Flakes
  • Minced Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Tofu

Recipe Instructions

  1. Chop up your kimchi and pork into bite sized pieces
  2. Heat a pot with some oil. Add kimchi, pork, red pepper flakes, and garlic
  3. Cook until the kimchi has softened and pork cooks
  4. Add water or broth and bring it to a boil and continue cooking for 10-20 minutes
  5. Drop in the tofu and wait until its cooked and tender
  6. Serve with red hot.
