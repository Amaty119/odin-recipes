Kimchi Fried Rice

A staple of modern Korean cuisine, Kimchi fried rice is easy to make and very tasty

Ingredients

  • Jasmine Rice
  • Kimchi
  • Spam
  • 2 Fried Eggs
  • Olive Oil
  • Sesame Oil
  • Sesame Seed

Recipe Instructions

  1. In a large pan, pour olive oil
  2. While you wait for the olive oil to heat, chop up your kimchi and spam into small slices
  3. Pour your kimchi and spam and stir.
  4. After 2-3 minutes, add in your rice.
  5. Stir and mix thoroughly until the rice has reddened.
  6. Pour in some sesame oil and crush in sesame seeds.
  7. Mix further until fully cooked.
  8. Serve with fried eggs.
